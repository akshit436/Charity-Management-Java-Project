Our Mission

"Helpage Saviours" invests in childhood — every day, in times of crisis and for our future. In the India and around the world, we give children a healthy start, the opportunity to learn and protection from harm. The children who cannot get basic necessities such as food, clothing, home education and employment are taken cared by our Organization. We help as many children as we can & provide them good life. The underprivileged children who are forced to beg money and do manual labor are provided with education so that they can live a good life in future. We can provide help to these children with the support and donations from people all over the world.....
